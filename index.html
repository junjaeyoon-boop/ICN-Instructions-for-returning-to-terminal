Transferring Terminal to Terminal

You may return to your designated terminal (T1/T2/Concorse) from your current location, but security regulations require complicated and rigorous procedures.

Please be sure to understand and follow the requirements below for safe and smooth transfer.

1. Staff Escort is required

According to the airport security regulations, the routes of departing passengers (3rd floor) and arriving passengers (2nd floor) are strictly separated. You have to pass through the entry floor to return to the passenger terminal, so you must travel with the guidance of the staff

2. Security Checks Conducted Twice

You will go through a total of two security checks during the procedure.

  • **1st security check:** When moving from current location, we will simply pass through without a thorough inspection of your belongings. (With Staff Escort)
  • **2nd security check:** After arriving at the destination passenger terminal, a thorough security check will be carried out again on all items you have. (Scan all belongings.)

3. Important: Carrying Any Liquid Exceeding 100ml 💧

Due to the second detailed security check, liquids in excess of 100ml (drink, cosmetics, etc.) are not allowed to be brought in If you have any, please consume or discard now before you go through the security check.

4. Duty Free Repackaging Guidelines

Unopened or unsealed duty-free items may become a problem during security screening. Repackaging may be required following staff guidance to prevent items from being confiscated.


It is a procedure for your safety, so we ask for your understanding and cooperation.
